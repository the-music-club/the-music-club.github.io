Meltdown - the place for rock, roll and all things metal.
This event is a safe haven for musicians with a taste for metal. Anyone can perform - be it in a solo performance or in a band - if you can shred, you are welcome to play anything you like.
Typically held in the Amphitheater, Meltdown is another flagship event of the music club, where we bring our best equipment for musicians to unleash their rock and roll.
We look forward to seeing the shred!
